## Contrast Phrases

| What most apps do | What Eskerra does |
|---|---|
| Store notes in a hidden database | Keep notes as plain files in your vault |
| Lock format to the platform | Keep Markdown readable anywhere |
| Make desktop and mobile the same | Give each device its own role |
| Require plugins to feel complete | Ship strong fundamentals |
| Slow startup before you can work | Open and capture immediately |
| Rewrite content silently | Save exactly what you wrote |
| Depend on a sync service | Work with any folder sync — or none |
| Grow features until it becomes a platform | Stay focused on writing and capture |
