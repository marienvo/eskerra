# The Eskerra Manifest

Software should help thought, not interrupt it.

Eskerra is built on a simple belief: your notes, ideas, plans, and writing should remain yours — ready when you need them, readable without the app, and free from software that outlives its usefulness.

---

## Ownership First

Your notes live as Markdown files in a folder you control.

Portable today. Readable in ten years. Open in any editor, moveable to any device, independent of Eskerra being installed.

Apps are temporary. Files can endure.

---

## Speed Is Respect

Waiting is friction. Friction taxes thinking.

Eskerra treats performance as a principle:

- fast startup
- fast note switching
- immediate search
- no layers between thought and text

The tool should feel ready before you need it.

---

## Desktop and Mobile Have Different Jobs

Most apps flatten every device into the same shape. The result is two mediocre experiences wearing the same coat.

Eskerra gives desktop and mobile genuinely different roles.

**Desktop is for depth:** writing, structuring, editing, developing ideas over time.

**Mobile is for motion:** capture, reading, quick review, light interaction.

Different contexts deserve tools built for those contexts.

---

## A Calm Place to Work

The core experience is complete without configuration.

No plugin setup before it feels right. No dashboard demanding to be filled. No feature surface competing with the editor for your attention. The environment is settled, clear, and ready.

Calm is not a lack of capability. It is a deliberate choice about where the attention should go.

---

## Markdown, Enhanced Carefully

Markdown is the source of truth, and that does not change.

Eskerra can make editing tables easier, frontmatter more approachable, and previews more helpful. The interface gets better. The file on disk stays clean, standard, and portable.

Better ergonomics. Honest format.

---

## Predictable by Design

The app behaves the way you expect it to.

Notes open consistently. Files save exactly what you wrote. Nothing rewrites content in the background or generates metadata you did not ask for. The behavior is transparent enough that you can trust it without thinking about it.

That reliability is the product.

---

## For People Who Take Their Work Seriously

Eskerra is for people who care about their files, value their time, and want a workspace that earns trust rather than demanding it.

Not a second brain as a service. Not a productivity platform. A fast, durable, focused environment for writing and thinking.

Eskerra is not trying to own your knowledge.

It is trying to deserve a place beside it.
