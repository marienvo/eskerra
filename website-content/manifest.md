# The Eskerra Manifest

Software should help thought, not interrupt it.

Eskerra is built on a simple belief: your notes, ideas, plans, and writing should remain yours — not rented from a platform, not trapped in a proprietary format, not slowed down by software that forgot its place.

---

## Ownership First

Your notes live as Markdown files.

Not inside a hidden database.  
Not behind an export button.  
Not inside a subscription-shaped vault.

Apps are temporary. Files can endure.

---

## Speed Is Respect

Waiting is friction. Friction taxes thinking.

Eskerra treats performance as a principle, not a decoration:

- fast startup
- fast note switching
- no unnecessary indexing rituals
- no sluggish layers between thought and text

The tool should feel ready before you need it.

---

## Desktop and Mobile Have Different Jobs

Many apps flatten every device into the same compromise.

Eskerra does not.

**Desktop is for depth:** writing, structuring, editing, processing ideas.

**Mobile is for motion:** capture, reading, quick review, light interaction.

Different contexts deserve different tools.

---

## Calm Over Chaos

No plugin circus required for the basics.  
No dashboard carnival.  
No feature confetti.

The core experience should already be strong, clear, and dependable.

---

## Markdown, Enhanced Carefully

Markdown remains the source of truth.

Interfaces may improve editing tables, frontmatter, previews, and workflows — but the underlying text stays portable and understandable.

Better ergonomics, not betrayal.

---

## Predictable by Design

No mysterious rewrites.  
No silent complexity.  
No hidden traps.

What the app does should make sense.

---

## For People Who Care

For people who value ownership, speed, clarity, durable formats, calm tools, serious writing, and systems they can trust.

Eskerra is not trying to own your knowledge.

It is trying to deserve a place beside it.
